## What It Is

**TrendMedium**: Dual moving average trend follower with 25/70 day MAs

- **Type**: Systematic trend following
- **Target**: Medium-term trends (2-4 months)  
- **Configuration**: 25-day fast MA, 70-day slow MA
- **Sharpe**: 0.638 (standalone), 0.792 (in portfolio)
