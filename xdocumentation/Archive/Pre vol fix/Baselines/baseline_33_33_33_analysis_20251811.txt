## Executive Summary

Analysis of three core trading sleeves reveals **dramatically regime-dependent performance**. Key finding: Strategies don't have universally low Sharpe ratios - they have regime-specific strengths and weaknesses.

**Critical Discovery:** The 2016-2019 Trade War Era represents a structural break where TrendImpulse v4 collapsed (-0.297 Sharpe, -28.48% drawdown) while TrendMedium and MomentumCore held up (+0.431 and +0.441 Sharpe). This explains IS/OOS instability.

**Portfolio Implication:** Equal-weighted allocation (33/33/33) achieves 0.665 Sharpe with low inter-strategy correlations (0.32-0.40), demonstrating clear diversification benefit.
